Trip to Wyoming

My fiance and I took a trip to Wyoming to visit Jackson Hole and the Grand Teton National Park in September 2020.

The airport in Jackson Hole, WY is directly next to the Teton Mountain Range. Usually this is a great view but the wildfires in California obscured the view.


We got settled and our first stop on our in the beautiful state of Wyoming would be an Eagle's bar for the game.


The next day we went out for our first hike around Jenny Lake. The morning was cold, around 35°F. By the afternoon it would be over 70°F and even 80°F some days.


The Jenny Lake trails offered some of the best views. Perfect for a Zoom background!


We got to plenty of wildlife on our trip. The bison were my favorite.


This is me sitting on a bear. We did not see a real bear on our trip but a women in the airport "traded" me her picture of a bear for a picture I had of a moose.


The vest I am wearing in this picture was bought for this trip and never worn again after the trip. I sometimes put it on and then decide not to wear it. I dont understand vest.
